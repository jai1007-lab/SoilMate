Our Impact

Our Approach

At ISAP, we are driven and motivated through the impact our work can bring to our target communities. We care deeply about real and tangible change and are committed to strenuous qualitative and quantitative measurement to assess if we are bringing effective and sustainable transformation to rural communities and also to understand how we can do better in everything that we do.

Internally, we invest in upskilling our own staff to be up-to-date on the latest best practices around impact measurement and on the ground we are committed to assuring impartiality of execution with avoidance of bias and detection of emerging challenges. We regularly submit our work to third party auditing to ensure that we are on track with our milestones and to understand how we can continuously improve and serve our famers and communities better.
